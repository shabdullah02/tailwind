About 1HORA

Our Story

1HORA has a 21,000 square meter production base and high-end production lines as well as a 1,200 square meter R&D laboratory and a team of more than 50 R&D experts. With the efforts of the team, 1Hora products have obtained more than 100 intellectual property rights and are widely used in digital products, electronic accessories, and lifestyle products. The products have evolved from focusing on 3C consumer electronics to diversifying. 1HORA has more than 1,000 distributors and more than 100 million global customers around the world, and its business covers more than 30 regions including Latin America, the United States, Africa, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and Europe. Step by step, 1HORA will slowly make electronic products affordable and durable life helpers for everyone.
